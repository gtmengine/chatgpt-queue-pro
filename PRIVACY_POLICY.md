# Privacy Policy - ChatGPT Queue Pro

**Effective Date:** November 14, 2025

**Last Updated:** November 14, 2025

## Overview

ChatGPT Queue Pro ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Google Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we may collect:
- **Basic Profile Information:** Name, email address, profile picture
- **Google Account ID:** Unique identifier for your Google account
- **Authentication Tokens:** Used to maintain your login session

### 2. Extension Usage Data
- **Prompt Chains:** Text prompts you create and save
- **Queue Statistics:** Number of prompts processed, success rates
- **Error Logs:** Technical issues encountered while using the extension

### 3. Technical Information
- **Browser Information:** Chrome version, operating system
- **Extension Usage:** Features accessed, frequency of use
- **Performance Metrics:** Extension response times, error rates

## How We Use Your Information

### 1. Authentication & Access
- Verify your identity for premium features
- Maintain secure login sessions
- Provide personalized user experience

### 2. Service Provision
- Process and queue your prompts
- Synchronize data across devices (future feature)
- Provide customer support

### 3. Improvement & Analytics
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Monitor extension performance

### 4. Communication
- Send important updates about the service
- Respond to your support inquiries
- Notify about new features or changes

## Information Sharing & Disclosure

### We MAY share information:
- ✅ **With Google:** For authentication purposes only

## Data Storage & Security

### Storage Location
- User authentication data stored securely with Google
- Extension preferences stored locally in your browser
- Usage analytics stored on secure servers (encrypted)

### Security Measures
- Industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure token storage and rotation
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

### Data Retention
- Authentication data: Stored until you revoke access
- Usage data: Retained for 24 months for analytics
- Deleted upon account deactivation or request

## Your Rights & Choices

### 1. Access & Control
- **View Account Info:** Access your profile information anytime
- **Download Data:** Request a copy of your data
- **Delete Account:** Remove all your data from our systems

### 2. Authentication Control
- **Sign Out Anytime:** End your session from the extension

- **Switch Accounts:** Sign in with different Google account

### 3. Data Preferences
